Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 24, 2026.

Elevare Studio ("Elevare", "we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect personal information when you visit https://www.elevares.ca, contact us, or book our real estate media services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly, including your name, phone number, email address, brokerage or business name, service address, and any project details you share with us through forms, calls, texts, or email.

We may also collect limited technical information automatically, such as IP address, browser type, device details, pages visited, referral source, and approximate location data used for analytics and site performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use personal information to respond to inquiries, provide quotes, schedule shoots, deliver media services, process payments through trusted third-party providers, send service-related updates, improve our website, and maintain business records.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only with service providers that help us run our business (for example, booking, hosting, analytics, invoicing, editing, and payment processing tools), or when required by law, legal process, or to protect our rights and safety.

4. Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to understand site traffic and improve user experience. You can control cookies through your browser settings, but disabling cookies may affect how some pages function.

5. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this policy, including client support, legal compliance, accounting, and dispute resolution requirements.

6. Data Security

We use commercially reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect personal information. No internet transmission or storage system is guaranteed to be 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may link to third-party websites or platforms. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies, and we are not responsible for the content or practices of those external services.

8. Your Rights and Choices

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information we hold about you, subject to legal and operational limitations. You can also opt out of non-essential communications at any time.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for business and professional use and are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When updates are made, we will post the revised version on this page and update the effective date above.

11. Contact Us

If you have any privacy questions or requests, please contact us at info@elevares.ca or call +1 (825) 883-1177.
